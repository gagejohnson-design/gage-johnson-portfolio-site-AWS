About

I bring a full-lifecycle perspective to enterprise technology: how software is sold, implemented, adopted, and trusted through reliable data and clear delivery. I’m a Data Management Consultant by trade, and I’m actively building full-stack and AWS fundamentals to deepen my technical execution.

My Path

I started in SaaS sales, where I learned how organizations evaluate solutions and what it takes to communicate value across business and technical audiences. From there, I moved into functional consulting and delivery, working closer to requirements, process alignment, testing cycles, and user enablement.

Today, I work in data management consulting—supporting enterprise implementations across multiple business units. I spend a lot of time improving data quality, validating migrations through QA cycles, and collaborating with developers, architects, and stakeholders to troubleshoot issues and keep delivery moving.

What I Do in My Role

My work is a mix of systems coordination and hands-on validation. I help translate business needs into clear documentation and actionable delivery steps, then support testing and readiness so teams can operate confidently after go-live.

I’ve contributed to stronger go-live outcomes by improving data quality and migration accuracy through validation and QA cycles, and I’ve built automation to structure large volumes of unorganized documentation—saving teams significant time and making deliverables easier to use.

What I’m Learning and Practicing

I’m building engineering fundamentals through bootcamp projects, side work, and consistent practice. My focus is on understanding application structure, working with APIs and databases, and improving my ability to build maintainable features end-to-end.

On the cloud side, I’m progressing through Adriaan Cantrill’s AWS Solutions Architect course and self-study, with hands-on labs to strengthen how cloud environments are configured, secured, and operated.

How I Work

I work top-down—aligning on business goals, user needs, and success criteria before execution. With a foundation in SaaS sales and functional consulting, I focus on bridging technical and non-technical teams, clarifying requirements early, and translating ambiguity into clear, actionable solutions.

As I continue building my skills in full-stack development and cloud, I apply the same mindset: move iteratively, validate assumptions, and prioritize quality and scalability. I approach problems from the end-user perspective, test functionality first, and use modern tools to troubleshoot efficiently—always focused on delivering solutions people actually adopt and organizations can grow with.
